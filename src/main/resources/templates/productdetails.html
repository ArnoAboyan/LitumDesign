Glass Bridge is an event inspired by the famous netflix series: Squid Game • The event consists of creating a glass bridge where players must jump over them and reach the finish line to be the winner of the game; • However, players will have some challenges for this. The event can have PVP combat during gameplay, as well as giving random weapons to players (set by config file) so they can kill each other; • In addition, it is possible to disable the PVP or even make it more hardcore, activating the system of dying after the fall;
